  • One famous interpretation of ResNet(Residual network) is to view the layer-to-layer transformation
    $$u_{n+1} = u_n + h \sigma (W_n u_n + b_n)$$
    as the discretization of a dynamical system(see Neural ODE). As a result, the process of training can be viewed as a numerical optimal control problem where weights and biases are control variables.
